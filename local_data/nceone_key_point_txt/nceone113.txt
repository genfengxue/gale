<k>fare n.

票价(尤其指交通),这里指请买票.

Train fares are going up again.
火车票价又要涨了.
</k>

</k>

6
I'll ask some of the `passengers`.
<k>passenger n.
['pæsɪndʒər]
乘客

Two passengers behind are talking loudly.
两位后座的乘客在大声地谈话.
</k>

8
I'm sorry. `I've got none.`
<k>I've got none.
我没有
none在这里指没有同一类的东西，注意与I've got nothing(我什么也没有)的区别.

-Do you have any cigarette?
-你有香烟么?
-I've got none.
-我没有(香烟,但有其他东西).

-Do you have any cigarette?
-你有香烟么?
-I've got nothing!
-我什么都没有!(没有香烟，也没有其他任何东西)
</k>

9
I haven't got any `either`.
<k>either adv.
也(否定句中)

I don't eat meat and my husband doesn't either.
我不吃肉,我丈夫也不吃.
</k>

10
Can you change this ten-pound note, `madam`?
<k>madam n.
['mædəm]
女士(对不认识的女性的敬称)

May I carry your cases for you, Madam?
我能为你提包吗,女士?
</k>


12
`Neither can I`.
<k>Neither + 助动词 + sb.
某人也没...

-Did you see it?
-你看见了吗?
-No. 
-没有.
-Neither did I.
-我也没有.
</k>


14
You must `get off` the bus.
<k>get off v.
下车
get on上车
get off/on一般与bus, train, boat and aircraft连用,如果是car用get in/into/out of the car

Shall we get off the bus now?
我们现在该下车了吧?
</k>

15
`None of` our passengers can change this note.
<k>none of...
一个也没...
两个都没有用neither, 超过两个都没有用none.

None of my children has blonde hair.
我的孩子(超过两个)没有一个是金黄头发的.
Neither of my parents likes my boyfriend.
我的父母(只有两个人)都不喜欢我的男朋友.
</k>

16
They're all `millionaires`!
<k>millionaire n.
[ˌmɪljə'ner]
百万富翁
同理, billionaire n. 亿万富翁

You want me to buy you a new car - do you think I'm a millionaire or something?
你想我给你买辆新车 - 你以为我是百万富翁还是什么的?
</k>

17
`Except` us.
<k>except prep./conj.
除了...之外

The museum is open daily except Mondays.
博物馆每天除了周一之外每天都开.
</k>

19
`So have I`.
<k>so + v. + sb.
某人也是.

-I like that actress.
-我喜欢那个女演员.
-So do I.
-我也喜欢.
</k>
